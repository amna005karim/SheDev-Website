Who we Are ?

SheDev is the first-ever Women lead tech company of Gilgit Baltistan. A platform that digitally empowers women, and provides them with opportunities to enhance and monetize their digital skills. The aim is to connect women to the digital, technologically advanced world, thus providing them with financial stability via atypical methods of learning and earning.
